A Comprehensive Digital Engagement & Authority Strategy with an emphasis on Reddit

Prepared For: California Superbike School (CSS) Leadership & Team

Prepared By: Eric “Rico” Oberndorfer assisted by an AI Strategic Assistant

Subject: Establishing Digital Authority, Driving Leads, and Streamlining Online Engagement via Content Leverage, Automation, and Authentic Interaction

1. Executive Summary:

California Superbike School stands as a global leader in motorcycle rider training, possessing unparalleled expertise, decades of data, and valuable educational content (books, videos). However, current digital challenges—including copyright infringement on YouTube, Reddit platform difficulties (shadowban, competitor presence), and limited team bandwidth—restrict CSS's ability to fully leverage its authority online. This proposal presents a robust, integrated strategy to solidify CSS's position as the premier and most helpful resource for performance motorcycle riding, with a primary focus on effectively engaging the crucial Reddit community while maximizing content synergy across all digital platforms.

The core strategy, "Automate Preparation, Humanize Engagement," focuses on building sustainable systems:

  • Resolving foundational issues (YouTube copyright, Reddit access).
  • Systematically organizing CSS's vast knowledge into a central, easily maintainable Knowledge Base (KB) using Google Sheets, potentially aided by AI for initial processing.
  • Implementing an automated workflow using Make.com and OpenAI (GPT) to monitor online discussions (primarily Reddit via Feedly), identify relevant questions, retrieve corresponding information and media links from the KB, and generate draft responses aligned with CSS's voice.
  • Establishing a strict Human Review & Posting Protocol where all AI-drafted responses are efficiently reviewed and edited for accuracy, tone, and authenticity by designated CSS team members, primarily Dylan Code, before being manually posted.
  • Integrating this core system with a synergistic, multi-platform social media strategy (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Discord) where content creation efforts (especially video) are strategically repurposed across channels, enhancing reach and ROI.

The work done for Reddit and YouTube directly fuels content for all other platforms. This approach dramatically increases efficiency, creating a sustainable, low-maintenance process that allows CSS to provide expert answers consistently without overwhelming the team.

The upfront investment in structuring knowledge and setting up automation builds a long-term asset, less dependent on specific short-term employee presence. It builds trust and authority, enhances brand visibility, protects intellectual property, and ultimately aims to increase course enrollments and sales of educational materials.

The estimated ongoing monthly cost for the essential software tools is approximately $38 - $48.

2. Problem Statement & Current Situation Analysis:

  • Intellectual Property Violation: Unauthorized distribution of CSS's full video content on YouTube devalues the product, confuses potential customers, and represents lost revenue/control.
  • Reddit Platform Barriers:
    • An existing account shadowban prevents any current engagement, blocking access to a key audience.
    • A competitor's employee moderating a major subreddit creates potential bias and complicates genuine participation.
    • Lack of time and a systematic approach prevents CSS from effectively monitoring relevant subreddits and responding to rider questions, leading to missed opportunities.
  • Suboptimal Resource Utilization: CSS possesses a "gold mine" of unique data, established teaching methodologies (from books/videos), and decades of experience that are not being systematically deployed to answer online questions or create engaging digital content efficiently.
  • Operational Constraints: The CSS team is small and extremely busy with core operations (running the school), limiting time for dedicated, consistent digital marketing efforts. A sustainable, efficient system is paramount.
  • Overarching Goal: CSS desires to establish a strong, authentic, and helpful online presence, particularly on Reddit, positioning itself as the go-to expert resource, driving business results without requiring unrealistic time commitments or outsourcing core engagement.

3. Proposed Overarching Strategy:

"Automate Preparation, Humanize Engagement" - Building a Sustainable System

This strategy balances the power of automation for efficiency with the necessity of human oversight for authenticity, quality, and strategic communication. Crucially, the significant upfront effort in building the Knowledge Base and setting up the automation creates a highly sustainable, scalable, and low-maintenance process. This system becomes a core operational asset, capable of consistently delivering value with minimal ongoing effort compared to purely manual approaches, and resilient to personnel changes. It ensures CSS's online interactions are timely, expert-driven, brand-aligned, and genuinely helpful.

Strategic Pillars:

  • Foundational Readiness & Asset Centralization
  • Intelligent & Efficient Monitoring
  • Automated Preparation Engine
  • Authentic Human Engagement & Quality Control
  • Synergistic Multi-Platform Content Strategy
  • Dedicated Community Hub (Discord)

Pillar Implementation Details

Pillar 1: Foundational Readiness & Asset Centralization

  • Action 1.1: Reclaim & Strategize YouTube IP
    • Task: Immediately file a DMCA Takedown Notice with YouTube for the stolen full video content.
      • Explanation: DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) (support.google.com) is the legal tool to remove infringing content.
    • Reasoning & Control: Reclaiming control allows CSS to:
      • Protect commercial value.
      • Control brand presentation.
      • Strategically use the full video: Upload it and meticulously timestamp key sections. These timestamps become direct links (MediaLink in KB) for Reddit/social comments, guiding users to the exact relevant moment.
      • Repurpose into micro-content: Systematically edit the full video into numerous YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok videos, and short clips for Reddit/Facebook posts, maximizing asset reach. This leverages the primary video asset across multiple platforms efficiently.
  • Action 1.2: Resolve Reddit Account Access & Define Persona
    • Task: Prioritize resolving the shadowban via the official Reddit Appeals page: (reddit.com/appeal). Be persistent, polite, identify CSS official representation (Dylan Code), state positive intent, seek resolution.
    • Explanation: Shadowbans render activity invisible; appeals are the only legitimate route.
    • Account Strategy Recommendation: Use named accounts linked to key personnel for a more human touch. Primary accounts for posting reviewed content:
      • Dylan_CSS (Owner & Chief of Marketing, high credibility)
      • Rico_CSS or alternative (Employee, relatable team member)
    • Reasoning: Named accounts foster better connection. Clear account ownership streamlines responsibility under Dylan Code's oversight. Legitimacy is key.
  • Action 1.3: Construct the Central Knowledge Base (KB) with AI Assistance
    • Tool: Google Sheets (sheets.google.com) - Create CSS_Knowledge_Base.
    • Columns: TopicID, PrimaryKeywords, SecondaryKeywords, ExplanationSnippet (Approved CSS explanation), MediaLink (Timestamped YouTube link, Short link, image URL, etc.).
    • Ingestion Process (Manual Core, AI Assist):
      1. Gather Sources: Digital books, video transcripts, notes, data summaries.
      2. AI-Assisted Processing: Use LLMs like ChatGPT (chat.openai.com), Google Gemini (gemini.google.com), or Claude (claude.ai) to accelerate initial processing. Prompt LLMs to summarize concepts, extract definitions/advice, suggest keywords, and rephrase technical explanations into concise snippets based on provided CSS source text.
      3. Human Review & Structuring: Dylan Code (or designated expert) must review all AI outputs for accuracy, nuance, and CSS methodology alignment. Edit rigorously. Organize validated information into the Google Sheet, assigning TopicIDs, confirming Keywords, finalizing ExplanationSnippets, and adding correct MediaLinks.
    • Explanation: The KB is the structured "single source of truth." High "KB Quality" (comprehensive, accurate, searchable) is critical for automation success. LLMs assist, but human expertise validates.
    • Reasoning: Centralizes knowledge, ensures consistency, provides structured data for automation, easily accessible/updatable. AI assist reduces initial burden.
  • Action 1.4: Refresh Existing Digital Assets
    • Task: Update YouTube (youtube.com/@superbikeschool), Instagram (instagram.com/superbikeschool/), and Facebook Page (facebook.com/californiasuperbikeschool) with current brand language, logos, banners, descriptions.
    • Reasoning: Ensures brand consistency before ramping up activity.

Pillar 2: Intelligent & Efficient Monitoring

  • Action 2.1: Implement Automated Monitoring
    • Tool Recommendation: Feedly Pro (feedly.com) (~$12/mo)
    • Task: Configure Feedly to track relevant subreddits (e.g., r/trackdays, r/motorcycles, r/NewRiders) and keywords (throttle, braking, vision, track day, cornering, lean angle, CSS, etc.). Obtain the Feedly RSS feed URL.
    • Explanation: Feedly aggregates mentions via RSS (a standard content feed format) for automation.
    • Reasoning & Benefit: Eliminates manual Reddit monitoring time drain. Captures opportunities efficiently, freeing up Dylan Code for review, strategy, and community management.

Pillar 3: Automated Preparation Engine

  • Tool Recommendation: Make (Core Plan) (make.com) (~$11/mo)
  • Task: Build automation Scenario in Make (Trigger via Feedly RSS -> Filter -> Parse Text -> Search Google Sheet KB -> Retrieve Snippet/Link -> Call OpenAI API -> Send Draft to Review Queue).
  • OpenAI API Prompt Refinement: Continuously refine the OpenAI prompt with CSS-specific language/phrasing examples to improve draft quality and tone alignment over time.
  • Reasoning: Automates repetitive retrieval/drafting based only on approved KB content. Make offers best cost-effectiveness and workflow power.

Pillar 4: Authentic Human Engagement & Quality Control

  • Action 4.1: Efficient Review Protocol
    • Task: Dylan Code (or a designated direct report if applicable) monitors the Review Queue (Slack/Email) at consistent, sustainable intervals (e.g., 2-3 scheduled blocks per day). Prioritize quality and sustainability over immediate response.
    • Task: Streamlined Review & Edit: Review every draft efficiently. Goal: Minimal edits (quick checks for accuracy, tone, relevance, compliance, minor personalization). If heavy rewriting is needed, refine the KB snippet or OpenAI prompt.
    • Reasoning: Balances responsiveness with realistic workload for Dylan Code. Focuses on efficient quality control.
  • Action 4.2: Manual Posting & Dedicated Device Strategy
    • Task: Post finalized text manually to Reddit using Dylan_CSS or Rico_CSS account from the Dedicated Posting Device.
    • Task: Implement Dedicated Posting Device: Procure a basic iOS/Android device + low-cost cellular plan, used exclusively for official CSS social media logins/posting (Reddit, IG, FB, TikTok, Discord).
    • Reasoning: Isolates activity, enhances security, prevents personal/official account mix-ups, creates focused workspace. Centralizes access while allowing secure remote use if needed.
  • Action 4.3: Streamlined Collaboration
    • Task: Primary review rests with Dylan Code. If input needed beyond KB, consult relevant expert directly. Coach involvement is opportunistic (e.g., specific AMAs), not required for daily flow.
    • Reasoning: Simplifies workflow, acknowledging primary responsibility rests with marketing leadership (Dylan Code).

Pillar 5: Synergistic Multi-Platform Content Strategy (Maximize ROI)

  • Core Principle: Content created once is repurposed strategically. The KB and YouTube video(s) are central assets.
  • Content Flow & Synergy:
    • KB Snippet/YouTube Clip: Used for Reddit reply -> Clip/Timestamp shared on Facebook -> Visuals become Instagram Reel/TikTok -> Concept becomes Discord discussion topic.
    • Vertical Video (TikTok/Reel): Uploaded as YouTube Short -> Shared across Stories -> Embedded in relevant blog post.
    • Reddit Engagement: Popular questions/discussions inspire future YouTube video topics, Instagram carousels, blog posts, and Discord AMA themes.
    • Themed Content: Plan content snippets (from KB, new recordings) for relevant themes (e.g., #TrackdayTuesday, r/NewRiders question days) across platforms.
  • Platform-Specific Content Expansion:
    • YouTube: Core technique clips/Shorts, Full video (timestamped), Tech Talks, Partner Spotlights, Influencer/Journalist Collabs, Track Guides (CSS lines), Live AMAs (Dylan/Keith/Coaches), Gear Guides.
    • Instagram/TikTok: Visual focus: action, quick tips, student successes, behind-the-scenes.
    • Facebook: Mix visuals with text updates, schedules, events, link sharing.
    • Blog (Website): SEO-focused articles expanding KB topics.
  • Reasoning: Integrated approach maximizes ROI on content creation efforts, builds a cohesive brand story, reaches diverse audiences, and creates a virtuous cycle where engagement on one platform informs content for others.

7. Risks & Mitigation:

Draft Quality:

Mitigation: Mandatory human review (Dylan Code); prompt refinement.

KB Gaps:

Mitigation: Ongoing KB updates; easy reporting mechanism for gaps.

Automation Errors:

Mitigation: Make monitoring/error handling.

Review Bottleneck:

Mitigation: Efficient workflow; realistic scheduling for Dylan Code; potential future delegation if needed.

Platform Rules:

Mitigation: Stay informed; prioritize value; manual posting + dedicated device reduces risk.

Community Management:

Mitigation: Clear Discord guidelines; start simple; potential future community mods.
