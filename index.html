Digital Engagement & Authority Strategy

Prepared By: Eric "Rico" Oberndorfer along with an AI Strategic Assistant.

1. Executive Summary

California Superbike School stands as a global leader in motorcycle rider training, possessing unparalleled expertise, decades of data, and valuable educational content (books, videos). However, current digital challenges—including copyright infringement on YouTube, Reddit platform difficulties (shadowban, competitor presence), and historically limited bandwidth for consistent digital engagement—restrict CSS's ability to fully leverage its authority online. This proposal presents a robust, integrated strategy to solidify CSS's position as the premier and most helpful online resource for performance motorcycle riding techniques, with a primary focus on effectively engaging the crucial Reddit community while maximizing content synergy across all digital platforms.

The core strategy, "Automate Preparation, Humanize Engagement," focuses on building sustainable systems:

  1. Resolving foundational issues (YouTube copyright, Reddit access).
  2. Systematically organizing CSS's vast knowledge into a central, easily maintainable Knowledge Base (KB) using Google Sheets, potentially aided by AI for initial processing.
  3. Implementing an automated workflow using Make.com and OpenAI (GPT) to monitor online discussions (primarily Reddit via Feedly), identify relevant questions, retrieve corresponding information and media links from the KB, and generate draft responses aligned with CSS's voice.
  4. Establishing a strict Human Review & Posting Protocol where all AI-drafted responses are efficiently reviewed, edited for accuracy, tone, and authenticity, and approved by Dylan Code (Owner & Marketing Lead) before being manually posted.
  5. Integrating this core system with a synergistic, multi-platform social media strategy (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Discord) where content creation efforts (especially video) are strategically repurposed across channels, enhancing reach and ROI. The work done for Reddit and YouTube directly fuels content for all other platforms.

This approach dramatically increases efficiency, creating a sustainable, low-maintenance process that allows CSS to provide expert answers consistently without overwhelming team capacity. The upfront investment in structuring knowledge and setting up automation builds a long-term asset, less dependent on fluctuating team availability. It builds trust and authority, enhances brand visibility, protects intellectual property, and ultimately aims to increase course enrollments and sales of educational materials. The estimated ongoing monthly cost for the essential software tools is approximately $28.

2. Problem Statement & Current Situation Analysis

  • Intellectual Property Violation: Unauthorized distribution of CSS's full video content on YouTube devalues the product, confuses potential customers, and represents lost revenue/control.
  • Reddit Platform Barriers:
    • An existing account shadowban prevents any current engagement, blocking access to a key audience.
    • A competitor's employee moderating a major subreddit creates potential bias and complicates genuine participation.
    • Historically, lack of time and a systematic approach prevents CSS from effectively monitoring relevant subreddits and responding to rider questions, leading to missed opportunities.
  • Suboptimal Resource Utilization: CSS possesses a "gold mine" of unique data, established teaching methodologies (from books/videos), and decades of experience that are not being systematically deployed to answer online questions or create engaging digital content efficiently.
  • Operational Constraints: The CSS team is lean and extremely busy with core operations (running the school), demanding highly efficient and sustainable systems for any new initiatives.
  • Overarching Goal: CSS desires to establish a strong, authentic, and helpful online presence, particularly on Reddit, positioning itself as the go-to expert resource, driving business results without requiring unrealistic time commitments or outsourcing core engagement.

3. Proposed Overarching Strategy: "Automate Preparation, Humanize Engagement" - Building a Sustainable System

This strategy balances the power of automation for efficiency with the necessity of human oversight for authenticity, quality, and strategic communication. Crucially, the significant upfront effort in building the Knowledge Base and setting up the automation creates a highly sustainable, scalable, and low-maintenance process. This system becomes a core operational asset, capable of consistently delivering value with minimal ongoing effort compared to purely manual approaches. It ensures CSS's online interactions are timely, expert-driven, brand-aligned, and genuinely helpful.

Strategic Pillars:

  • Pillar 1: Foundational Readiness & Asset Centralization: Address critical blocking issues and systematically organize core knowledge assets for long-term use.
  • Pillar 2: Intelligent & Efficient Monitoring: Systematically identify relevant online conversations without manual time drain.
  • Pillar 3: Automated Preparation Engine: Leverage technology to process information and draft initial responses based only on approved CSS knowledge.
  • Pillar 4: Authentic Human Engagement & Quality Control (Centralized Oversight): Ensure all external communication is efficiently reviewed, refined, approved, and posted under the direction of Dylan Code.
  • Pillar 5: Synergistic Multi-Platform Content Strategy: Maximize the value of content creation by repurposing assets across YouTube, Reddit, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Discord.
  • Pillar 6: Dedicated Community Hub (Discord): Foster a vibrant, controlled community space for students, coaches, and enthusiasts.

Pillar 1: Foundational Readiness & Asset Centralization

Action 1.1: Reclaim & Strategize YouTube Intellectual Property

  • Task: Immediately file a DMCA Takedown Notice with YouTube for the stolen full video content. (Link to YouTube Copyright/DMCA Info)
  • Reasoning & Control: Reclaiming control enables CSS to:
    • Protect paid product value.
    • Control brand presentation.
    • Strategically use the full video: Upload it (decision needed: Public with CTAs, Unlisted, or Members-Only) and meticulously timestamp key sections. These timestamps become direct MediaLinks in the KB for Reddit comments.
    • Repurpose into micro-content: Systematically edit the full video into numerous YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok videos, maximizing the asset's reach.

Action 1.2: Resolve Reddit Account Access & Define Persona

  • Task: Prioritize resolving the shadowban via the official Reddit Appeals page: https://www.reddit.com/appeal. (Persistent, polite appeals identifying CSS official representation).
  • Account Strategy Recommendation: Use named accounts linked to key personnel. Primary posting account operated under Dylan Code's direction. Suggestions:
    • Dylan_CSS (Owner/Marketing Lead, high credibility)
    • Rico_CSS (Employee, relatable team member - posts approved by Dylan)
  • Reasoning: Named accounts enhance connection. Clear oversight by Dylan Code ensures consistency and brand alignment. Legitimacy is key.

Action 1.3: Construct the Central Knowledge Base (KB) with AI Assistance

  • Tool: Google Sheets (Link: sheets.google.com) - CSS_Knowledge_Base
  • Columns: TopicID, PrimaryKeywords, SecondaryKeywords, ExplanationSnippet (Approved CSS explanation), MediaLink (Timestamped YouTube link, Short link, image URL, etc.).
  • Ingestion Process (Manual Core, AI Assist):
    1. Gather Sources (digital books, transcripts, notes, data).
    2. AI-Assisted Processing: Use LLMs (ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude) to accelerate summarization, keyword extraction, and initial snippet drafting via targeted prompts (e.g., "Summarize core concepts...", "Suggest keywords...", "Rephrase explanation...").
    3. Human Validation & Structuring: Dylan Code or designated expert *must review* all AI outputs for accuracy and alignment with CSS methodology. Edit rigorously. Organize validated info into the Google Sheet, assigning TopicIDs, finalizing ExplanationSnippets, adding MediaLinks.
  • Explanation: The KB is the structured "single source of truth." LLMs speed up processing, but human validation ensures KB quality (accuracy, searchability, correct links), which is critical for automation success.
  • Reasoning: Creates a consistent, scalable foundation. AI assistance reduces initial setup burden. Google Sheets offers free, accessible management.

Action 1.4: Refresh Existing Digital Assets

  • Task: Update existing YouTube (www.youtube.com/@superbikeschool), Instagram (https://www.instagram.com/superbikeschool/), and Facebook Page (https://www.facebook.com/californiasuperbikeschool) with current brand language, logos, banners, descriptions under Dylan Code's direction.
  • Reasoning: Ensures brand consistency before increasing activity.

Pillar 2: Intelligent & Efficient Monitoring

Action 2.1: Implement Automated Monitoring

  • Tool Recommendation: Feedly Pro (Link: feedly.com) (~$12/mo)
  • Task: Configure Feedly to track relevant subreddits and keywords (e.g., throttle, braking, vision, track day, cornering, lean angle, CSS, superbike school). Obtain the RSS feed URL for Make.
  • Explanation: Feedly acts as an automated listening post, aggregating mentions based on your rules and feeding them into the automation engine via RSS.
  • Reasoning & Benefit: This eliminates manual Reddit monitoring time drain, freeing up Dylan Code for strategic tasks, review, and content creation. Ensures relevant conversations are captured efficiently.

Pillar 3: Automated Response Preparation Engine

  • Tool Recommendation: Make (Core Plan) (Link: make.com) (~$11/mo)
  • Task: Build the Make Scenario (Trigger via Feedly RSS -> Filter -> Parse Text -> Search Google Sheet KB -> Retrieve Snippet/Link -> Call OpenAI API -> Send Draft to Review Queue).
  • OpenAI API Prompt Refinement: Continuously refine the prompt sent to OpenAI under Dylan Code's guidance to improve draft quality and tone alignment with the CSS brand voice.
  • Reasoning: Automates repetitive info retrieval and initial drafting based *only* on approved KB content. Make offers the best cost-effectiveness and power.

Conceptual Flow Diagram (Text Representation):

Feedly detects new Reddit mention -> Sends data via RSS.
Make Scenario Triggered by new RSS item.
Make filters item for relevance? (If irrelevant -> Stop).
Make parses the Reddit user's text.
Make searches Google Sheet KB for keywords? (If no match -> Notify/Stop).
Make retrieves `ExplanationSnippet` & `MediaLink` from Sheet.
Make sends Context + Snippet + Link to OpenAI API with refined prompt.
OpenAI generates draft response based on prompt & info.
Make sends Context + AI Draft + Snippet + Link to Review Queue (e.g., Slack).
(Manual Step Begins) CSS Team Member (Dylan/Delegate) reviews queue.
(Manual Step) Edit/Approve Draft.
(Manual Step) Post finalized response to Reddit.

Pillar 4: Authentic Human Engagement & Quality Control (Centralized Oversight)

Action 4.1: Efficient Review Protocol (Dylan Code Oversight)

  • Task: Dylan Code (or delegate under his direct supervision) monitors the Review Queue (Slack/Email) at consistent, sustainable intervals (e.g., 2-3 times daily). Focus on quality and sustainability.
  • Task: Streamlined Review & Edit: Review every draft. Aim for minimal edits (accuracy, tone, relevance check + personalization) due to quality KB/prompts. If heavy edits are needed, improve the source KB snippet or OpenAI prompt.
  • AI Voice Training (Future Option): Fine-tuning LLMs is a Phase 3+ optimization possibility. Start with strong prompt engineering; human review remains essential.
  • Reasoning: Centralizes final approval with Dylan Code, ensuring brand consistency and quality. Balances responsiveness with realistic workload via efficient review process.

Action 4.2: Manual Posting & Dedicated Device Strategy

  • Task: Post the finalized, edited response manually to Reddit from the designated account (Dylan_CSS or Rico_CSS under Dylan's approval) using the Dedicated Posting Device.
  • Task: Procure and set up a basic, dedicated iOS/Android device with its own low-cost cellular plan exclusively for official CSS social media account logins and posting.
  • Reasoning: Isolates activity (security, avoids IP/fingerprinting issues), creates focus, centralizes access securely. Manual posting ensures compliance.

Action 4.3: Streamlined Collaboration

  • Task: Dylan Code handles primary review/approval. If deeper technical input is needed beyond the KB, Dylan consults directly with Keith Code or relevant experts. Coach involvement is *opportunistic* (e.g., specific AMAs) rather than part of the core review workflow.
  • Reasoning: Simplifies workflow, acknowledging Dylan's central role and expertise, and adapting to realistic coach availability.

Pillar 5: Synergistic Multi-Platform Content Strategy (Maximize ROI)

  • Core Principle: Content created once is repurposed strategically across platforms. The KB and YouTube video(s) are central assets.
  • Content Flow Examples:
    • Video Clip: A 3-minute YouTube clip explaining trail braking -> Becomes a `MediaLink` in KB -> Used in Reddit replies -> Clip shared on Facebook -> Key visual moments edited into an Instagram Reel/TikTok video -> Concept discussed further in a Discord AMA.
    • Short Vertical Video: A quick tip filmed for TikTok/Reels -> Uploaded as YouTube Short -> Potentially embedded in a relevant blog post -> Shared on Facebook/Instagram Stories.
    • Reddit Themed Days: Identify popular themes (e.g., #TrackdayTuesday, `r/NewRiders` Q&As). Plan content snippets (text, short video, image) from the KB or new recordings specifically for these days to increase relevance and visibility.
    • Data Insights: Unique CSS data -> Becomes an infographic for Instagram/Facebook/Blog -> Explained in a short YouTube video -> Referenced in technical Reddit discussions.
  • Platform-Specific Content Expansion:
    • YouTube: Beyond core technique clips/Shorts: Tech Talks, Partner Spotlights, Influencer/Journalist Collaborations, Track Guides (showing lines/reference points), Live AMAs, Gear Guides.
    • Instagram/TikTok: Focus on visually engaging, quick-hitting content: action shots, quick tips, student successes, behind-the-scenes.
    • Facebook: Mix visuals with text updates, course schedules, event promotion, link sharing.
    • Blog (Website): Longer-form articles expanding on KB topics for SEO.
  • Reasoning: Ensures maximum return on content creation effort, builds a cohesive brand narrative, reaches diverse audiences effectively.

13. Risks & Mitigation

  • Inaccurate/Robotic Drafts: Mitigation: Mandatory Dylan Code review/approval; continuous prompt refinement.
  • KB Gaps/Errors: Mitigation: Ongoing KB updates overseen by Dylan; easy process for team to flag gaps.
  • Automation Errors: Mitigation: Regular Make monitoring; build simple error handling.
  • Review Bottleneck: Mitigation: Streamlined review process focused on efficiency; Dylan manages workflow; realistic scheduling.
  • Platform Rule Changes: Mitigation: Stay informed; prioritize value; manual posting + dedicated device reduces risk.
  • Community Management Burden (Discord): Mitigation: Clear guidelines; Dylan oversees management (potential delegation to Rico); start simple, scale features.
